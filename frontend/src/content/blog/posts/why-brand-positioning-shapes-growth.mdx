---

Brand positioning influences how a business is understood, remembered, and compared.

A business may offer a strong service, but if its value is not clearly positioned, the market may not recognise its difference.

Good positioning helps answer:
- What do we want to be known for?
- Why should clients choose us?
- How do we want to be perceived?

When positioning is clear, marketing becomes sharper, sales conversations improve, and the brand becomes easier to trust.
